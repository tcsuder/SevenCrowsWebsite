Assisting authors with their publishing needs

From manuscript to marketing

Hello! I’m Kerry Darrington. I’m a certified author’s assistant, the owner of Seven Crows Virtual Services, a writer and a recent self-published author. I have had a love affair with books since early childhood. After a decade in management, where I’d find myself in the nearest bookstore practically every day on my lunch break, I decided it was time to start my own business helping authors and aspiring authors to make their publishing dreams a reality and to surround myself with books full time. Over the past few years I have helped others go from a concept for a book to a finished product, and for me few things are more rewarding! Fortunately, with close to twenty years of sales experience, I have the skills available to make sure the author’s masterpiece gets marketed and sold successfully as well.


In my spare time, you can find me roaming the beautiful mountains of Montana with my husband and our handsome pup, Grizwold; doing yoga, feeding my TED Talk addiction, visiting local breweries, writing poetry and curled up with a good book (of course!).

How can a virtual assistant help me?

As a published or pre-published author, you know the tremendous amount of work that goes into writing and publishing a book. There is a lot to learn about this process, and it’s constantly evolving. Self-publishing, or finding a commercial publisher, can be a lengthy and tedious process, but it doesn’t have to be! If you have partnered with a VAA (virtual author’s assistant) before, hopefully you have experienced the great amount of relief that comes from having a skilled professional take on the time consuming tasks that make-up the authoring process. In addition to getting your book published, you must then focus on the marketing and selling piece, which can be a full time job in itself!


Working with a professional VAA can save you time and money, and when you need that extra push, encouragement or inspiration, we are here to help with that, too. We have a great understanding of the publishing industry, with valuable resources and connections. We are aware of the right steps you should take in the publishing process, and in what order, and we can make sure they are executed correctly and seamlessly. We can help get your manuscript into the right hands, and help you learn the pitfalls to avoid. Once your book is published, we can then help you to market it and grow your author platform; both pivotal steps in becoming a successful author today.
